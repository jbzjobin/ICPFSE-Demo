Our Ministries

Transforming communities by reaching the next generation where they are.

AWAKE

Youth Camps

Awake

Awake youth camps are transformative experiences designed to ignite a passion for Christ in the hearts of young people. Through dynamic worship, powerful messages, and intentional small groups, students encounter God in a profound way, leading to spiritual renewal and life-changing decisions.


Our camps provide a safe space away from the distractions of everyday life, allowing students to focus on their relationship with God and build lasting friendships with like-minded peers.

ASPIRE

Campus Ministry

Aspire

Aspire focuses on reaching students right where they are: in their schools and colleges. We believe the campus is one of the most critical mission fields today.


By empowering student leaders to start campus clubs, host Bible studies, and engage in meaningful conversations, Aspire equips young believers to be the light of the world and make a tangible impact on their peers.

CONNECT

One-Day Conferences

Connect

Connect conferences serve as powerful catalysts for launching and sustaining local ministries. These one-day events gather youth from across a city or region for concentrated times of worship, equipping, and vision-casting.


It's about bringing the local body of Christ together, breaking down walls, and uniting under a shared purpose to see our cities transformed by the Gospel.

MEDICAL

Global Outreach

Medical Mission

Faith without works is dead. Our Medical Mission ministry provides an opportunity for students and professionals to serve communities in need around the world.


Through medical camps, health education, and compassionate care, we demonstrate the love of Jesus in practical ways, addressing both physical and spiritual needs in underserved areas.
