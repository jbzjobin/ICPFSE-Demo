Who We Are

For over two decades, we have been walking alongside young people —
equipping them not just to survive, but to lead.

PURPOSE

Our Mission

Called to Raise Leaders.
Committed to the Next Generation.

We are a nationwide network of chapters — each one a local home for faith, friendship, and formation. From quarterly meetups to national conferences, we create spaces where young people are spiritually grounded, personally developed, and sent out as leaders in their communities. No chapter looks the same, because no community is the same — and we honour that.

Our Vision

A Generation Fully Alive in Their God-Given Purpose

We dream of an America where every young person — regardless of background or city — is rooted in faith, clear in their identity, and bold enough to change the world around them.

LEGACY

Our Journey

Early 1980s

The Spark in India

A vision to reach students with the gospel ignited on college campuses across India, and a movement was born.

Late 1980s–1990s

Crossing Oceans

ICPF graduates carried the flame to America, refusing to leave their mission behind when they moved.

1997

ICPF USA is Born

Prayer groups scattered across the country came together, and ICPF USA was officially established to fuel campus ministry through prayer and action.

2001

Awake Ignites

The first Awake Camp opened its doors in Waxahachie, TX — and a generation began to wake up to their calling.

2010

Ignite Launches

IGNITE was created to reach middle schoolers, planting biblical roots before the world tries to pull them up.

Today

Expanding Nationwide

Active campus groups are running in Houston, Dallas, and beyond — with a vision to plant in every major university in America.
