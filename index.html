NATIONWIDE

A Nation of Chapters.
One Heartbeat.

We are a nationwide family — chapters rooted in cities from coast to coast, each one a local home for the ICPF mission. Through quarterly gatherings, virtual sessions, regional camps, and national conferences, we create spaces for growth, mentorship, and lifelong connection. No two communities are the same, and we embrace that — tailoring every program to serve the people God has placed in each city.
